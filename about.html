About Me

Making Cloud Computing Simple

Hi, I'm Sandesh from Tech With Sandesh. I help professionals learn DevOps and AWS by breaking down complex concepts into simple, practical lessons.

My Journey in DevOps & Cloud

I started my career as a system administrator and gradually transitioned into DevOps and cloud engineering. Throughout my journey, I realized that many resources online were either too technical or lacked practical, real-world context.

This inspired me to start teaching—to bridge the gap between theory and practice. My goal is to make DevOps and AWS accessible to everyone, whether you're just starting out or looking to level up your skills.

Today, I've helped thousands of learners understand cloud computing through simplified explanations, hands-on projects, and real-world scenarios that actually matter in production environments.
