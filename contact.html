Before You Reach Out

Quick answers to frequently asked questions

What's the best way to get started?

Start with the free resources on my Resources page. Download the DevOps Roadmap and explore the guides. When you're ready for structured learning, check out the AWS Networking Masterclass.

Do you offer one-on-one coaching?

Currently, I focus on group masterclasses and courses to help more people. However, I'm available for corporate training and consulting. Email me for details.

Can I suggest a topic for future content?

Absolutely! I love hearing what you want to learn. Send me an email or message on LinkedIn with your suggestions.

Are you available for speaking engagements?

Yes! I'm open to speaking at conferences, meetups, and corporate events about DevOps and AWS. Contact me via email with event details.

How can I collaborate with you?

I'm always interested in collaborating on educational content, tools, or community initiatives. Reach out via email with your proposal.
